About

I'm a postdoctoral scholar at Stanford's Rivas Lab (Department of Biomedical Data Science), where I build novel computational genomics methods and AI-native clinical systems. I'm also a physician with over a decade of clinical experience in the ER.

Before Stanford, I co-designed the core phenotyping infrastructure for FinnGen, defining disease endpoints across Finnish national health registries for 500K+ individuals — infrastructure now used by thousands of researchers globally. My research spans large-scale biobank genetics, pharmacogenomics, and computational methods for biomedical discovery.

My current focus is building systems that let scientists collaborate with frontier AI models to accelerate biomedical research by an order of magnitude. I believe the future of biology requires comprehensive world models that integrate genomic, clinical, and environmental data — and I'm building toward that future.

Projects

UltraLasso
GPU-accelerated genomics

A GPU-native pipeline that runs complete genome-wide association studies in minutes instead of hours or days. Makes biobank-scale genetic analysis fast enough to run interactively alongside AI coding agents.

View project
VISTA Architect
Clinical AI platform

An ARPA-H funded AI-native clinical platform deployed at Stanford Medicine. Transforms electronic health records into structured knowledge graphs and lets clinicians chat with full patient histories — validated in oncology tumor board workflows.

Watch demo video
HGI
Individual genome analysis

A personal genome analysis tool that delivers a full clinical-grade interpretation of an individual's whole genome in under 10 minutes, with a conversational "talk to your genome" interface.

View on GitHub
Killbench
Clinical AI safety benchmark

An adversarial benchmark that tests whether LLMs can make safe clinical decisions under uncertainty. Reveals concrete failure modes in frontier models that standard medical benchmarks miss.

Contact for details
MOA Dreamer
LLM memory architecture

A compartmentalized architecture that gives frontier LLMs fast access to newly learned skills without retraining the main model. Provides large accuracy improvements to Claude and GPT on tasks designed to defeat standard retrieval.
